Medical Movement Sensor

As a software engineering , and as a researchers, we learned to ask the right questions to make theory to reality. We used the software and technology to create a tool that can help to a big part of general population. The purpose of the project is to provide information for a person with movement disorders in their hands (or anyone who wants to be aware to the level of stability of his hands). The most common movement disorder is known as tremor, which can appear at rest, movement or static state The app uses a sensor accelerator that measures mobility and unique for Android - checked about 400 Numbers for instability.
